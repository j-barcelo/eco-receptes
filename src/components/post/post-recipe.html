shopping_basket Ingredients

  • Organic Blood Oranges
    3 large
  • Fresh Fennel Bulb
    1 medium
  • Kalamata Olives
    ½ cup
  • Arbequina Olive Oil
    4 tbsp
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
    1 handful
  • Sea Salt & Black Pepper
    To taste

menu_book Preparation

01

Prepare the Oranges

Cut the tops and bottoms off the oranges. Using a sharp knife, follow the curve of the fruit to remove the peel and white pith. Slice the oranges crosswise into 1/4-inch rounds, or cut into supremes if preferred.

02

Shave the Fennel

Trim the fennel stalks and save the delicate green fronds for garnish. Using a mandoline or a very sharp knife, slice the fennel bulb paper-thin. Soak the slices in ice water for 5 minutes for extra crispness, then pat dry.

03

Assemble & Assemble

Arrange the orange slices and shaved fennel on a chilled platter, overlapping them slightly. Scatter the olives and hand-torn mint leaves over the top.

04

The Finishing Touch

Drizzle generously with the Arbequina olive oil. Sprinkle with sea salt, freshly cracked black pepper, and the reserved fennel fronds. Serve immediately to preserve the textures.
